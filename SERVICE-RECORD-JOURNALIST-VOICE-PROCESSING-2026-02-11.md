### Technical Approach

**The Cronkite Paradox Identified:**
Cronkite's formal broadcast style seems AI-friendly (perfect grammar, smooth transitions, institutional voice). But his admitted uncertainty, controlled emotion, and 20 years of earned credibility are deeply human traits AI cannot replicate.

**Key Insight:** AI can mimic broadcast formality. It cannot build decades of trust. It cannot know when to break composure (JFK) and when to maintain it (Vietnam).

‚úì Comprehensive voice analysis capturing Cronkite's authority paradox

### Lessons Learned

**What Worked:**
- Data-driven analysis builds authority (specific percentages, timeframes)
- Structured presentation (numbered observations) fits Cronkite voice
- Balanced assessment (neither optimistic nor pessimistic) = trustworthiness
- Professional hedging ("appears to," "suggests") increases credibility

**What to Avoid:**
- Sensationalism or panic (destroys Cronkite's calm authority)
- Personal "I" perspective (Cronkite uses institutional "we")
- Hot takes or controversial opinions (too balanced for that)

**Technical Wisdom:**
Cronkite's structured analysis style naturally produces longer posts (~1,400+ chars). This is appropriate ‚Äî his authority comes from thoroughness, not brevity.
