Von Manstein is one of the most successful propaganda operations in modern military history. He transformed himself from a convicted war criminal (9 guilty verdicts) actively complicit in the Holocaust into the "best general of WWII" through systematic fabrication and strategic omission. This case demonstrates how Cold War politics enabled a comprehensive rehabilitation built on documented lies.

**Critical finding**: Von Manstein signed an order calling for "harsh punishment of Jewry" (Nov 20, 1941), then testified under oath 5 years later that he knew nothing about anti-Jewish operations. This is not ambiguity or interpretation - this is **direct, documented fabrication**.

## Pattern 5: Strategic Omission (Primary Pattern)

### What Von Manstein Omitted at Nuremberg (August 10, 1946)

#### Omission 1: The November 20, 1941 Order

**What he signed**:
> "The soldier must appreciate the necessity for the harsh punishment of Jewry, the spiritual bearer of the Bolshevist terror."

> "Jewry is the middleman between the enemy at our rear...occupies all key posts of the political leadership and administration...forms the nucleus for all disquiet and possible revolts."

**What he testified 5 years later**:
> **DR. LATERNSER:** Did you get to know that the aim of partisan warfare was to exterminate the Jews and Slavs?
>
> **VON MANSTEIN:** No.

**Analysis**: This is not a failure of memory. This is a calculated lie. The order he signed explicitly targets Jews for "harsh punishment" using classic antisemitic propaganda (Jews as "Bolshevist terror," "middleman between enemy"). At Hamburg trial (1949), when confronted with this order, he claimed "no recollection of signing it" - despite the document bearing his signature.

**Severity**: EXTREME - Direct documentary contradiction of sworn testimony.

---

#### Omission 2: Wehrmacht Direction of Einsatzgruppen Operations

**Ohlendorf testimony (January 3, 1946)** - 7 months before von Manstein testified:

**Distance regulation order**:
> "An order from the 11th Army was sent to Nikolaiev that liquidations were to take place only at a distance of not less than two hundred kilometers from the headquarters of the commanding general."

**Simferopol acceleration order**:
> "In Simferopol the army command requested the Einsatzkommandos in its area to hasten liquidations, because famine was threatening and there was a great housing shortage."

**Von Manstein's claim**:
> "We soldiers did not wage this ideological war. In my opinion it was waged by Hitler together with some of his closest collaborators, and a limited number of accomplices."

**Analysis**: Von Manstein testified AFTER Ohlendorf. He knew this testimony existed. He chose to omit that:
1. His 11th Army regulated WHERE liquidations occurred (200km rule)
2. His 11th Army ordered liquidations ACCELERATED (Simferopol)
3. His 11th Army sent observers to witness executions
4. 90,000 people were murdered in his operational area during his command

The "apolitical soldier" narrative is built on strategic silence about documented operational cooperation.

**Severity**: EXTREME - Omitted evidence that directly contradicted his core narrative.

#### Omission 3: Pre-Campaign Briefing on Liquidation Mission

**Ohlendorf testimony**:
> "Himmler told me that before the beginning of the Russian campaign Hitler had spoken of this mission to a conference of the army groups and the army chiefs...and had instructed the commanding generals to provide the necessary support."

**Von Manstein's claim**:
> "At that time what did you imagine would happen when a person was handed over to the SD?"
>
> "It was our impression that first of all the SD would interrogate such a person and then probably send him to some camp."

**Analysis**: Army commanders were briefed PRE-CAMPAIGN on the liquidation mission. Von Manstein's claim of ignorance ("probably send him to some camp") is contradicted by Ohlendorf's testimony that Hitler personally briefed commanding generals on providing support for Einsatzgruppen operations.

**Severity**: EXTREME - Claimed ignorance of mission he was briefed on before the campaign began.

**Status**: Requires Tier 1 source verification, but cited in multiple academic works.

His memoir, published 2 years after prison release, became a Cold War bestseller and the foundation of his rehabilitation. It systematically omits:

1. **November 20, 1941 order** - not mentioned
2. **Einsatzgruppe D operations** - not mentioned
3. **90,000 victims in his operational area** - not mentioned
4. **Hamburg trial conviction on 9 war crimes charges** - minimized as "victor's justice"
5. **Simferopol "hasten liquidations" order** - not mentioned
6. **Wehrmacht-Einsatzgruppen cooperation agreement** - not mentioned

**Instead, the memoir presents**:
- "Military genius" thwarted by Hitler's strategic incompetence
- "Apolitical soldier" who focused on operational excellence
- Victim of unjust trial influenced by Soviet propaganda
- Wehrmacht fought clean war, SS/Hitler fought ideological war

**Analysis**: The memoir is a comprehensive rewrite of history. Every incriminating piece of evidence from Hamburg trial is omitted or reframed. The result: Western audiences read a tale of military brilliance and political persecution, with zero mention of Holocaust involvement.

**Severity**: EXTREME - Systematic omission of all Holocaust-related evidence from 500+ page memoir.

## Pattern 3: Fabrication (Secondary Pattern)

### Fabrication 1: "I Disobeyed the Commissar Order"

**Von Manstein's claim (Nuremberg, Aug 1946)**:
> "I said to myself that as a soldier I could not possibly co-operate in a thing like that, and I told the commander of the army group under which I came at the time, as well as the commander of the armored group, that I would not carry out such an order, which was against the honor of a soldier."

> "In practice, the order was not carried out."

**Contradiction**:
> "Documents from 1941 presented at Nuremberg and at Manstein's own later trial contradicted Manstein's claims, showing he received regular reports throughout that summer regarding the execution of hundreds of political commissars."

**Hamburg trial verdict**: GUILTY on "Execution of commissars" charge.

**Analysis**: Von Manstein claimed principled refusal to execute commissars. Trial documents proved his 11th Army DID execute commissars. He was convicted of this crime at Hamburg. The claim of disobedience is a fabrication designed to establish moral credibility.

**Severity**: HIGH - Fabricated moral stand contradicted by documentary evidence and guilty verdict.

---

### Fabrication 2: "I Didn't Know Einsatzgruppen Aimed to Exterminate Jews"

**Von Manstein's claim (Nuremberg, Aug 1946)**:
> **DR. LATERNSER:** Did you get to know that the aim of partisan warfare was to exterminate the Jews and Slavs?
>
> **VON MANSTEIN:** No.

**Contradictory evidence**:
1. **Pre-campaign briefing** (per Ohlendorf): Hitler briefed army commanders on liquidation mission
2. **November 20, 1941 order** (his signature): Calls for "harsh punishment of Jewry"
3. **Simferopol order** (per Ohlendorf): Army command requested "hasten liquidations"
4. **90,000 killed** in his operational area over 9 months

**Analysis**: It is implausible to claim ignorance when:
- You were briefed on the mission before the campaign
- You signed an order targeting Jews for "harsh punishment"
- Your army regulated where liquidations occurred (200km rule)
- Your army ordered liquidations accelerated (Simferopol)
- 90,000 people were murdered in your operational area

This is not ignorance. This is fabrication.

**Severity**: EXTREME - The ignorance claim is contradicted by his own signed orders.

### Fabrication 3: "We Soldiers Did Not Wage This Ideological War"

**Von Manstein's claim (Nuremberg, Aug 1946)**:
> "We soldiers did not wage this ideological war. In my opinion it was waged by Hitler together with some of his closest collaborators, and a limited number of accomplices."

**Contradictory evidence**:
1. **Wehrmacht-Einsatzgruppen cooperation** (per Ohlendorf): Formal agreement, army provided movement/supply/operational areas
2. **Army operational control** (per Ohlendorf): "Army was also entitled to issue orders to the Einsatzgruppen if the operational situation made it necessary"
3. **November 20, 1941 order**: Von Manstein's own order uses ideological language ("Jewry...spiritual bearer of the Bolshevist terror")
4. **Simferopol order**: Army command requested acceleration of ideological killings

**Analysis**: The "clean Wehrmacht" myth - military war vs ideological war - is a fabrication. Von Manstein's 11th Army actively directed ideological operations. His November 20 order proves he personally framed military operations in ideological terms (Jews as Bolshevist threat).

**Severity**: EXTREME - The core narrative ("apolitical soldier") is contradicted by his own orders and Wehrmacht operational cooperation.

## Clean Wehrmacht Myth Architecture

Von Manstein was a KEY ARCHITECT of the Clean Wehrmacht myth. Here's how he built it:

### Phase 1: Nuremberg Defense Document (August 1946)

**Document**: 132-page defense of Wehrmacht General Staff/OKW
**Authors**: Von Manstein + General Westphal
**Audience**: International Military Tribunal
**Purpose**: Establish that Wehrmacht fought professionally, SS/Hitler fought ideologically

**Key arguments**:
1. Wehrmacht objected to criminal orders (e.g., Commissar Order)
2. Einsatzgruppen operated independently under SS command
3. Army commanders were ignorant of atrocities
4. Wehrmacht was bound by military obedience, not Nazi ideology

**Result**: Wehrmacht leadership acquitted at Nuremberg (though individuals convicted at subsequent trials).

---

### Phase 2: Nuremberg Testimony (August 10, 1946)

**Role**: Defense witness
**Strategy**: Reinforce 132-page document with personal testimony
**Narrative**: "I disobeyed criminal orders, I didn't know about Holocaust, I fought a military war"

**Impact**: Established template for Wehrmacht commanders' defense at subsequent trials.

---

### Phase 3: Hamburg Trial Defense (1949)

**Defense strategy**:
- Contextualize orders within "exigencies of Eastern Front combat"
- Argue orders were "standard Wehrmacht practices for rear-area security"
- Claim orders did NOT "explicitly authorize racial killings or exceed military necessity"
- Question whether Hague Convention applied to Soviet Union

**Result**:
- Convicted on 9 "military" war crimes (commissars, POWs, scorched earth)
- Acquitted on 3 Holocaust-related charges ("insufficient direct linkage")

**Propaganda value**: Acquittal on Holocaust charges enabled claim of persecution, not accountability.

---

### Phase 4: Cold War Rehabilitation (1949-1953)

**Political context**:
- Berlin Blockade (1948-49)
- NATO formation (1949)
- Korean War begins (1950)
- West German rearmament debate

**Elite support**:
- **B.H. Liddell Hart** (British military historian): Public campaign for release, 1949-1953
- **Winston Churchill**: Pressured British government for early release
- **Konrad Adenauer**: Needed Wehrmacht generals for Bundeswehr

**Narrative**: "Brilliant strategist unjustly imprisoned by victor's justice, needed for Cold War defense"

**Result**: Released after 4 years (of 18-year sentence) on "health grounds."

---

### Phase 5: *Lost Victories* (1955)

**Publication**: 2 years after release, German original
**English edition** (1958): Foreword by B.H. Liddell Hart
**Length**: 500+ pages
**Content**: Operational history, strategic analysis, deflection of war crimes charges to "victor's justice"
**Omissions**: ALL Holocaust evidence (November 20 order, Einsatzgruppen cooperation, Hamburg convictions)

**Reception**: Bestseller, embraced by Western military establishment as authoritative account of Eastern Front.

**Result**: Von Manstein transformed from convicted war criminal to respected military thinker.

## Why This Propaganda Succeeded

### Factor 1: Hamburg Trial Acquittal on Holocaust Charges

The tribunal's acquittal on 3 Holocaust-related charges (despite conviction on 9 other war crimes) gave von Manstein legal cover to claim:
- "Unjustly accused of Holocaust involvement"
- "Victim of Soviet propaganda"
- "Vindicated by British military court"

**Reality**: Tribunal found "insufficient direct linkage" - a HIGH evidentiary bar, not exoneration of involvement.

**Propaganda use**: Von Manstein treated acquittal as proof of innocence, not legal technicality.

---

### Factor 2: Strategic Omission is Hard to Detect

General readers of *Lost Victories* had NO access to:
- Ohlendorf testimony (1946)
- Hamburg trial transcript (1949)
- November 20, 1941 order
- Wehrmacht-Einsatzgruppen cooperation documents

**Result**: Without primary sources, readers couldn't detect omissions. Memoir appeared comprehensive.

**Only specialists knew**: Scholars with access to Nuremberg documents and Hamburg trial records knew the omissions, but their work was not widely read.

---

### Factor 3: Cold War Framing

Anti-communist narrative made Wehrmacht generals USEFUL to the West:
- Soviet Union = existential threat
- Wehrmacht fought Soviets = valuable experience
- Holocaust = Nazi regime crime, not Wehrmacht crime
- Rehabilitation = pragmatic necessity for Cold War defense

**Result**: Political incentives aligned to accept von Manstein's fabrications rather than investigate them.
