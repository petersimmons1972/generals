## Lessons for AI Historical Research System

### Pattern 5 Detection Method (PROVEN):

**Step 1**: Read subject's account (memoir, testimony)
**Step 2**: Read contradictory primary sources (enemy testimony, trial documents)
**Step 3**: Document WHAT IS OMITTED from subject's account
**Step 4**: Assess whether omissions are incidental or systematic
**Step 5**: If systematic, classify as Pattern 5 (Strategic Omission)

**Von Manstein exemplar**: EVERY piece of Holocaust evidence omitted from 500-page memoir = Pattern 5 EXTREME.

---

### Red Flag Checklist (VALIDATED):

- ✅ Self-authored memoir → Tier 7 propaganda, not Tier 1 evidence
- ✅ Post-war trial acquittal → Read full verdict + evidence, not just outcome
- ✅ Elite endorsements → Political utility ≠ moral vindication
- ✅ "Best of" superlatives → Propaganda RED FLAG, require neutral comparison
- ✅ Systematic omissions → Pattern 5 indicator, compare to primary sources
- ✅ Cold War rehabilitation → Political context explains acceptance of fabrications

---

### Tier System (REFINED):

**Tier 1** (highest): Primary sources contradicting self-interest
- Example: Ohlendorf testimony (condemns himself AND Wehrmacht)
- Reliability: 95-100% for facts stated

**Tier 2**: Self-interested testimony under oath
- Example: Von Manstein Nuremberg testimony (defends Wehrmacht)
- Reliability: 20-30% for claims, 80% for facts admitted against interest

**Tier 3**: Contemporary official documents
- Example: Hamburg trial verdict
- Reliability: 70-90% depending on political context

**Tier 7** (lowest): Self-authored memoirs
- Example: *Lost Victories*
- Reliability: 0-20% for claims, read for WHAT IT OMITS not what it proves
