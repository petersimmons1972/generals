### Beda Fomm Battle (February 5-7, 1941) - VERIFIED

**British forces**:
- 4th Armoured Brigade: 40-50 cruiser tanks, 80 light tanks
- By battle end severely depleted:
  - 3rd Hussars: 4 cruisers, 24 light tanks
  - 7th Hussars: 1 cruiser, 20 light tanks
  - 2nd Royal Tanks: 10 cruisers, 7 light tanks
- **Source**: Australian Official History (FULL ACCESS)
- **URL**: https://www.ibiblio.org/hyperwar/UN/Australia/Bengazi/Benghazi-11.html
- **Reliability**: Tier 3 (contemporary Allied official document)

**Italian losses**:
- 20,000 prisoners (final surrender)
- 112 medium tanks captured (newly-arrived)
- 216 guns captured
- 1,500 wheeled vehicles
- General Tellera killed in battle
- General Bergonzoli captured
- "All that was left of the Italian Tenth Army" destroyed
- **Source**: Australian Official History (FULL ACCESS)
