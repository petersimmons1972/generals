### O'Connor Command Confirmation

**Line 5184-5186**:
> "Western Desert Force commanded by Lieut.-General R. N. O'Connor® was presently reinforced by the 7th Royal Tank Regt with Infantry tanks, or T' tanks as they were always called."

**Footnote®**: "General Sir Richard O'Connor, G.C.B., D.S.O., M.C."

**Context**:
- O'Connor commanded Western Desert Force
- Reinforced with 7th Royal Tank Regiment (Infantry/I tanks)
- End of September 1940: 7th Indian Infantry Brigade (Brig. H.R. Briggs) arrived
- British battalion: 1st Royal Sussex Regiment
- Indian battalions: 4th Sikh Regiment, 4/16th Punjab Regiment
