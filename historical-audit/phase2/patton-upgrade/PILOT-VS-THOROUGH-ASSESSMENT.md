### Tier 3: PILOT ERROR DETECTED

| Pilot Claim | Thorough Finding | Error Type |
|------------|------------------|------------|
| Slapping quote from "Patton's diary" | **Quote NOT in diary, from Farago biography** | SOURCE MISATTRIBUTION |

**Pilot stated**:
> "Patton wrote in his diary that he 'gave him the devil, slapped his face with my gloves and kicked him out of the hospital' (re: Kuhl)"

**Thorough finding**:
- Accessed December 1943 diary entries (retrospective references to August incidents)
- Diary language: "I had been dealing with heroes. I saw two men whom I thought were cowards. Naturally I was not too gentle with them."
- Quote attributed to Patton does NOT appear in accessible diary text
- Quote appears in Ladislas Farago's *Patton: Ordeal and Triumph* (biography)

**Impact**: Pilot relied on Farago's biography without noting intermediary source. Quote may exist in investigation records, but pilot's attribution to "diary" is incorrect.

**Correction required**: Change citation from "Patton's diary" to "investigation records cited by Farago" or remove quote until primary source verified.

## Key Lessons

### What Works Well

1. **Library of Congress digitization**: Patton's diaries fully accessible, transcribed, searchable
2. **Open-access scholarship**: War on the Rocks, Military Wiki, institutional timelines
3. **Cross-verification**: Multiple sources for statistical claims
4. **Honest limitation documentation**: Explicit about what is/isn't accessible

### What Blocks Progress

1. **Commercial archives**: Third Army AAR costs money
2. **Academic paywalls**: University presses, HarperCollins
3. **Institutional restrictions**: 403 errors on Army/Air Force papers (unclear why)
4. **Specialized scholarship**: Barbarossa analysis not in general sources

### Methodology Validation

**Thorough standard is EFFECTIVE**:
- Upgrades pilot from secondary to primary sources
- Detects errors through direct comparison
- Provides evidence chains and quotes
- Documents limitations honestly

**Thorough standard is HONEST**:
- Does not claim comprehensive coverage when paywalls block access
- Distinguishes between verified and cited claims
- Acknowledges unverifiable specifics

**Thorough standard is FEASIBLE**:
- Completes in 1-2 days as predicted
- Maximizes public/open-access sources
- Produces substantially improved accuracy (90 → 92-95)
