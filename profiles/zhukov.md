## World War II Achievements

Georgy Zhukov was the Soviet Union's greatest military commander — the man who stopped the German advance, organized the counteroffensives, and led the final assault on Berlin.

**Career highlights:**
- Battle of Khalkhin Gol (1939): Defeated Japan in undeclared border war
- Defense of Moscow (1941): Stopped German advance at the gates of Moscow
- Battle of Stalingrad (1942-43): Co-planned Operation Uranus, encircling 300,000 German troops
- Operation Bagration (1944): Largest Allied offensive of WWII
- Battle of Berlin (April 1945): Commanded the assault that ended the war in Europe

**Leadership style**: Brutal efficiency. Achieved military objectives regardless of cost. Brilliant operational art — the "deep battle" doctrine of simultaneous attacks across the entire front.
