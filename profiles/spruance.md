## World War II Achievements

Raymond Spruance won the pivotal Battle of Midway despite being a cruiser admiral thrust into a carrier command. He won not through boldness — but through disciplined risk calculation.

**Career highlights:**
- Battle of Midway (June 1942): Launched strike at maximum range against Japanese carriers; sank all 4 in a single day
- Battle of the Philippine Sea (June 1944): Destroyed Japanese naval air power decisively
- Commanded Fifth Fleet through Iwo Jima and Okinawa landings
- Consistently achieved strategic objectives through deliberate, calculated execution

**Leadership style**: Methodical, analytical, taciturn. Studied every variable before acting. "The Quiet Warrior" never sought the spotlight — let results speak.
