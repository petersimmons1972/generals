## World War II Achievements

Ernest King rebuilt the U.S. Navy after Pearl Harbor and commanded it through total victory. He was described as "the most even-tempered man in the Navy — always in a rage." His clinical precision forged a fighting fleet.

**Career highlights:**
- Led US Navy as COMINCH and CNO simultaneously (unprecedented dual command)
- Directed the Battle of the Atlantic anti-submarine campaign
- Oversaw Pacific strategic direction alongside Nimitz
- Managed the largest naval expansion in history
- Presided over victory in both Atlantic and Pacific theaters

**Leadership style**: Clinical precision. Zero tolerance for sloppiness. Demanded exact execution. Managed via data and clear directives. Uncompromising standards.
