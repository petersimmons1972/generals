## World War II Achievements

Chester Nimitz took command of the shattered Pacific Fleet on December 31, 1941 — three weeks after Pearl Harbor. He inherited catastrophe and forged victory.

**Pacific Campaign highlights:**
- Battle of Midway (June 1942): Decisive intelligence-driven victory — sank 4 Japanese carriers
- Island-hopping campaign across the Central Pacific (1943-1945)
- Approved Operation Downfall planning and prepared for the invasion of Japan
- Presided over Japanese surrender aboard USS Missouri (September 2, 1945)

**Leadership style**: Strategic patience. Rebuilt the Pacific Fleet through calm, systematic organizational mastery. Delegated authority clearly. Maintained high-trust relationships with commanders. Never micromanaged.
