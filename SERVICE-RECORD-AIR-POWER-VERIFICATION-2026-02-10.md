## Recommendations for Future Operations

### Process Improvements

1. **Always mandate independent verification**: "Verify these traits" should be standard, not "confirm these traits"
2. **Encourage challenge**: Explicitly reward historians who find contradictions vs. rubber-stamping
3. **Require nuanced analysis**: Single-word traits often oversimplify complex personalities
4. **Document limitations**: Include "avoid deployment for" sections as Harris profile demonstrated
5. **Cross-verify mythology**: Popular figures (Puller, Mitchell) require extra scrutiny vs. hagiography

### Research Quality Standards

1. **Primary sources preferred**: Museum archives, contemporary accounts, official records
2. **Multiple source triangulation**: Single-source claims should be flagged
3. **Distinguish observation from interpretation**: "Less charismatic" is interpretation, "deliberately low-profile" is observed behavior
4. **Quantitative evidence valued**: LeMay's SAC metrics (888 ‚Üí 43,722 runs) strengthen claims
